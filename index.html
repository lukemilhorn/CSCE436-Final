Team 6 - I Don't Know Anyone in this Class

Final Term Project

#Motivation To limit the spread of COVID and other pathogens by flipping the norm of an everyday object, the keyboard and mouse, to help users identify peripherals that are unclean.

#5 Week Milestones Week 1: Come up with the initial idea, sketch out a use case. Week 2: Come up with a name and finalize the concept. Iron out any roughnes with the idea. Week 3: Refine idea with feedback from Professor. Low fidelity cardboard prototype. Week 4: Work out bugs of low fidelity prototype. Begin 3D prototyping the design and system. Week 5: Create video demonstrating the use case and situation the keyboard would be used in.

#Team Members Luke Milhorn, Nathaniel Singewald, James Cuaderes, Fernando De La Fuente, Andrew Plaisance
