Team 6 - I Don't Know Anyone in this Class

Final Term Project

Motivation

To limit the spread of COVID and other pathogens by flipping the norm of an everyday object, the keyboard and mouse, to help users identify peripherals that are unclean.

Weekly Milestones

Week 1: Come up with the initial idea and sketch out a use case.
Week 2: Come up with a name and finalize the concept. Iron out details and any roughness with the idea.
Week 3: Refine idea with feedback. Create a low fidelity cardboard prototype.
Week 4: Work out any bugs of low fidelity prototype. Begin 3D prototyping the design and system.
Week 5: Create video demonstrating the use case and situations the keyboard would be used in.
